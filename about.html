After graduating American University in Washington, DC with a degree in communications, Ariana worked in Public Relations for a few years. Upon a return to the Detroit area in 2006, she began working Property Management for nearly 10 years. When Lyft started in the Detroit market, Ariana was one of the first drivers on the road and has maintained a 4.9-5 star rating over nearly 11,000 rides given in five years. In her time at Lyft, she was one of the first members of Lyft's Driver Advisory Council, representing the Midwest.
