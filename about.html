After graduating from American University in Washington, DC with a degree in communications, Ariana worked in Public Relations for several years. Upon returning to the Detroit area in 2006, she worked in property management for nearly 10 years.

When Lyft came to the Detroit market, Ariana was one of the first drivers on the road and has maintained a 4.9-5 star rating over nearly 11,000 rides given in five years. She was one of the first members of Lyft's Driver Advisory Council, representing the Midwest.

Connecting with so many people in and around Detroit, exposed Ariana to the technological revolution occurring in the area. She attended Grand Circus' after hours bootcamp, 3 nights a week, from July through December 2018. She enjoyed creating pos sites, linking to RESTful APIs for movie searches and weather results, and connecting with others looking to embark on a journey into web development. The bootcamp experience ignited Ariana's desire to continuously learn and adapt with new technologies.
